Stay at our fantasy styled bed and breakfast.

Step beyond the ordinary and into a realm of legend. At Dragon’s Keep, weary travelers, brave adventurers, and noble guests alike will find warmth, comfort, and a touch of magic. Whether you seek rest after a long journey or a story worth telling, your stay begins here.

About the Keep

Dragon's Keep is a fantasy-themed bed and breakfast built for travelers who want more than a normal stay. Our inn combines the comfort of a cozy retreat with the atmosphere of an old castle, complete with stone halls, warm candlelight, themed rooms, and weekend events.

What Guests Can Expect

  • Fantasy-themed rooms with unique designs
  • A warm lobby inspired by medieval inns
  • Weekend events, feasts, stories, and activities
  • Quiet spaces for relaxing, reading, or planning your next adventure
  • Friendly service for travelers, families, and adventurers alike

Featured Stay
