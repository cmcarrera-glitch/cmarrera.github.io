A Noble Stay for Brave Travelers

The Knight's Chamber is a warm and noble room designed for honored guests, wandering champions, and weary adventurers. With sturdy stone walls, rich wooden furnishings, and a welcoming atmosphere, this chamber offers both comfort and strength.

Guests of the Knight's Chamber can enjoy a restful night's sleep after long journeys, quests, or tournaments. This room captures the spirit of courage, honor, and old-world comfort.
