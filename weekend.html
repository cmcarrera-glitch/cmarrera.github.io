Weekend Events at Dragon's Keep

Every weekend, Dragon's Keep welcomes travelers to enjoy food, stories, games, and magical entertainment inside our fantasy-themed inn.

Friday Night Feast

Enjoy a medieval-style dinner with warm bread, roasted meats, sweet cider, and music from traveling bards.

Saturday Quest Hunt

Search the grounds, solve clues, and complete a light-hearted adventure inspired by legends of the keep.

Sunday Story Circle

Gather near the hearth for tales of dragons, lost kingdoms, brave knights, and mysterious magic.
